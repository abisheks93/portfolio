My Project

Human Following Robot

The Human Following Robot is an innovative project designed to automatically detect and follow a person in real time using sensor-based technology. By employing devices such as ultrasonic or infrared sensors and integrating them with an embedded control system, the robot can maintain a safe distance while adjusting its speed and direction according to the user’s movement. This functionality makes it suitable for applications like personal assistance, shopping carts, and elderly support, where hands-free operation is highly beneficial. The project highlights the effective use of robotics, automation, and intelligent control to enhance human-machine interaction.
